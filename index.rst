If you forget your TaxSlayer Pro Login/My Account details, you can quickly recover your username or reset your password safely through the official login page. Follow the steps below to regain access.

---

Step-by-Step Recovery Guide
---------------------------

1. **Go to the official TaxSlayer Pro Login/My Account page**  
   Visit `https://www.taxslayerpro.com/login`.

2. **Click “Forgot Username or Password?”**  
   This link is located below the login form.

3. **Enter your registered email address**  
   Use the email linked to your account.

4. **Check your email for instructions**  
   Follow the recovery link provided.

5. **Reset your password**  
   Log in using the new credentials.

6. **Enable two-factor authentication (optional but recommended)**  
   For extra security.
